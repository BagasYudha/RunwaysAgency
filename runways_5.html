Interview with Soyi, Runways model

Jakarta, Okt 20, 2023 — In an exclusive interview with Soyi, the rising star of the runway, we delve into her journey, challenges, and aspirations in the fashion industry. Soyi, known for her unique style and confident presence, has quickly become a sought-after model, gracing the catwalks of prestigious fashion weeks worldwide.

During our conversation, Soyi shared insights into her beginnings, highlighting the pivotal moments that propelled her career. From her early days of auditioning to her breakthrough moment at a major fashion show, Soyi's story is one of resilience and determination. She spoke passionately about the importance of staying true to oneself and the role of diversity in fashion, advocating for more representation and inclusivity on the runways.

Soyi also touched on the challenges she faces, including the intense competition and the pressures of maintaining a public image. Despite these hurdles, she remains grounded, crediting her support system of family and friends for her continued success.

Looking to the future, Soyi expressed her desire to expand her influence beyond modeling. She envisions herself as a mentor for aspiring models and hopes to collaborate with designers to create her own fashion line, emphasizing sustainable and ethical practices.

This interview provides a glimpse into Soyi's world, offering inspiration to those who aspire to follow in her footsteps. As she continues to break barriers and set new standards in the fashion industry, Soyi's journey is a testament to the power of perseverance and authenticity.
